Yuye Peng

Co-founder of Orca Design Studio, Yuye Peng is a versatile professional with over 7 years of experience as a Product Designer, also experienced as a Web Developer, and an Architectural Designer.

With a deep understanding of design trends and user needs, Yuye Peng leverages extensive research and user data to craft intuitive, compelling designs. Her experience includes roles at top tech companies like Adobe and Stash, as well as contributions to award-winning architecture firms such as OMA and Henning Larsen. This unique blend of architectural insight and tech industry expertise enables her to deliver innovative design solutions for a diverse audience.

Haitao Zhou

Co-founder of Orca Design Studio, Haitao Zhou is a seasoned architect with over a decade of experience, also skilled as a Web Developer.

Currently is a Registered Architect in the State of New York, Haitao's background includes working at award-winning architecture firms such as GLUCK+ and Ennead. He has designed, managed, and executed a diverse range of projects, including single-family homes, multifamily housing, schools, laboratories, and commercial buildings. His extensive experience and versatile skill set enable him to deliver innovative and well-executed design solutions.
